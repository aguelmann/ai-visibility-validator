Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI visibility?

AI visibility refers to your website's ability to be accessed, parsed, and referenced by AI search engines and Large Language Models (LLMs) like ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity.

Why does speed matter more for AI than traditional SEO?

AI systems face a timeout wall ‚Äî if your site doesn't respond in 1-2 seconds, it's treated as unavailable (not just ranked lower). After passing that wall, race conditions determine ranking among fast sites.

What is the Chrome UX Report?

CrUX is a dataset containing real user experience measurements from millions of websites. It provides real-world data on how fast sites actually load for users.

Is this tool really free?

Yes, completely free with no signup required. The Chrome UX Report API is free, and I'm committed to keeping this tool accessible to everyone.

What if my site has no CrUX data?

CrUX requires a minimum threshold of real-user visits. If your site doesn't have data, it means either it's too new or doesn't have enough traffic to be measured yet.

What's the target TTFB for AI visibility?

First, stay under 1-2 seconds to pass the timeout wall. Then aim for under 600ms for optimal AI crawler access. Under 200ms is ideal and wins most race conditions.
